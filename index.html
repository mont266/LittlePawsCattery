About us

Our Story

At Little Paws Cattery, we've been caring for cats since 2022. Our passion for feline companionship and commitment to providing exceptional care has earned us a reputation as a 5-star rated cattery in Coleraine, Northern Ireland. We are experienced cat lovers and we always go above and beyond to ensure that every cat in our care is happy, healthy, and loved.

Our Facilities

Our cattery boasts top-notch facilities that provide a comfortable and safe environment for your feline friends. We have full-sized walk-in pens which provide a safe and secure area for your pets. We also maintain a strict cleaning and disinfection regimen to ensure the health and wellbeing of all our guests.

Our Philosophy

At Little Paws Cattery, we believe that every cat deserves the best care possible. That's why we strive to provide a stress-free, comfortable, and enjoyable experience for every cat in our care. We believe that cats thrive on individual attention and affection, and we make sure to give each and every cat the love and attention they need to feel at home. Our philosophy is simple: happy cats make happy owners.
