Terms and Conditions of Little Paws Cattery
1. Vaccination Requirements:

All cats must be fully vaccinated against Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Feline Calicivirus, Feline Leukaemia Virus & Feline Panleucopaenia.

Proof of inoculation within the last 12 months must be produced on the day of arrival.

The proprietor reserves the right to refuse admission to any animal that is not fully vaccinated.

2. Insurance:

Your pet is fully insured during its stay at Little Paws Cattery.

However, insurance does not cover veterinary fees incurred for conditions prevailing prior to boarding.

3. Pet Condition:

All pets must be clean and in good condition.

Your account will be charged if it is necessary to use worming/flea treatments.

4. Rates and Charges:

Rates are on a daily basis, including the day of arrival and departure.

This is to allow for essential disinfecting and preparation of the room.

However, there is no charge for the day of collection if your pet is collected before 12 noon.

Payment is expected in full on the day of collection.

In the case of long-term boarders, accounts will be paid monthly, and a deposit will be required.

5. Health and Admission:

No cat suffering from, or suspected to be suffering from, any infectious or contagious disease can be accepted.

All cats will be examined on arrival.

Therefore, the proprietor reserves the right to refuse admission to any cat showing any signs of ill health, pending the advice from a veterinary surgeon.

Owners will appreciate that this is essential in order to safeguard the health of all the cats boarded.

6. Early Collection:

Should you collect your cat before the due date of departure, you will be charged for the full period booked.

7. Liability:

Whilst every care and precaution is taken during boarding, responsibility can only be accepted at the owner's risk.

8. Unclaimed Pets:

If your pet is not collected within two weeks of the scheduled collection date, and if no contact can be made with the owner, Little Paws Cattery will presume ownership and re-home accordingly.
